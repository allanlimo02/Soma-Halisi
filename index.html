About Soma Halisi

For a long period of time, consumers have been buying counterfeit books from random distributors. These books are not sourced directly from the publishers. This normally reduces the income of both the publishers and the authors. Also, most of these books are substandard, in that, they might have several pages missing, and also they are not durable due to the type of materials used to make plagiarized books.

If this stated problem is not solved, the plagiarism will affect both the Authors, publishers and consumers. The authors will have reduced income because we shall have so many pirated versions of the same book in the market. Publishers will lose trust from the consumers, due to the poor quality of books available in the market. They will also lose revenue, due to the availability of cheap books of the same version in the market. Consumers on the other hand will be victims of substandard books and hence losing money to pirated books.
